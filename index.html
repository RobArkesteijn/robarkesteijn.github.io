-Travelling can be stressful-

Sometimes, while travelling, instead of having the time of your life with family or friends, you might experience a really stressful time. Because you don't want anything to go wrong. For example, you go to the airport to get your flight to your next destination, but the airport is really crowded. Therefore there are long lines at the check-in desk and afterwards there is a really long waiting line to go through customs. Once it is finally your turn, the next task is at hand. Should you keep your belt on, should you keep your shoes on? How about my watch, or my necklace. Every airport is different, what do they expect from you this time. If you managed to get passed this obstacle you head towards the right gate, where the boarding will soon start, if you managed to make it in time afcourse. Everything seems to have gone well, but.. isn't your carry-on bag to heavy or to big? Do you still have your boarding pass? Or what about your passport, you definitly can not lose that.



-Handling new situations-

Travelling doens't have to be stressful at all. That is afcourse if you're well prepared but also if you have the right mindset. Travelling to a destination that you have never been to before is always more of a challenge than going to a familiar place. It doesn't matter how well prepared you are, there will be situations that you did not anticipate, and that is perfectly fine. Travelling is about discovering new places and cultures, it shouldn't be the case that you already know everything before arriving at your destination. If something doens't go your way, there are always plenty of people around who are willing to help you.
